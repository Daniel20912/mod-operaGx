Um mod feito por um fã de uma das melhores obras do terror e dos games, Silent Hill 2,
contém sound effects do jogo original do Playstation 2 que foram usados para os sons do navegador
como sons de abrir e fechar guias e sons do teclado, as cores foram baseadas na capa do jogo e um
video de wallpaper com imagens do jogo. As músicas são a White Noiz, Promise (Reprise), A World of
Madness e a Theme of Laura.

A mod created by a fan of one of the best works of horror and gaming, Silent Hill 2, 
contains sound effects from the original PlayStation 2 game that were used for browser 
sounds like tab opening and closing sounds and keyboard sounds. The colors were based 
on the game cover, and there is a video wallpaper with images from the game. The music 
includes White Noiz, Promise (Reprise), A World of Madness, and the Theme of Laura.
